Voice identity for messaging.

Emojis and stickers can’t carry real identity or nuanced emotion. Voice can — but today’s messaging makes voice hard to edit, hard to reuse, and hard to trust.

IdentityVoice lets people send AI-edited emotional voice messages tied to a reusable Voice ID, so your voice becomes a real identity layer inside chat.

What we build

Consumer app + messaging integration for expressive, editable voice messages and persona voice identity.

Why now

Voice AI is mature, but messaging UX is stuck. We bring voice identity to the most frequent daily behavior: chat.

Status

MVP built (iOS). Domain email + authentication set up. Now shipping the public landing page + onboarding.
